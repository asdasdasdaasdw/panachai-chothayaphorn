Assetto Corsa

Assetto Corsa is my go-to for realism and customisation. The modding scene is incredible — I can grab a small mod and totally change how the game feels. I love testing different cars and tuning setups that make each session feel unique.

F1 25

F1 25 is more arcade-leaning but perfect for playing with friends. My favourite mode is Co‑Op Career, where me and a mate take on objectives and build rivalries. I also spend time in Time Trial leaderboards to see how I stack up online.

Minecraft & Roblox

Sandbox games like Minecraft and Roblox let me build, explore, and create. They’re relaxing and fun, and I love the endless possibilities.
